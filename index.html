About the Workshop

Built for working designers

This isn't a beginner's intro to Figma. It's a focused, practical workshop for designers who already ship work — and want to ship better work.

We'll cover the decisions that separate good design from great design: how to structure process, communicate with stakeholders, and make visual choices that hold up under real-world constraints.

You'll leave with frameworks you can apply immediately, not theory you'll forget next week.
